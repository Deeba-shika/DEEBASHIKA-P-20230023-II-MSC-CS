                                                                                          PROJECT  :  PHOTOGRAPHY ..!

ABSTRACT:
                 
                   This project showcases a newly designed photography website featuring a user-friendly interface and comprehensive functionality. 
The site includes key sections such as a Home page, Capture page, History page, and About page, each tailored to enhance the user experience. 
The Home page serves as an introduction, while the Capture page highlights a curated selection of reference images taken by the photographer. 
The History page provides insights into the evolution of the work, and the About page offers background information on the photographer. 
Overall, the website is crafted to present a visually appealing and informative platform for showcasing and exploring photographic work.
